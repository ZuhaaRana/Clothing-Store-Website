Online Orders


Order on our website by placing your item to ‘Add To Shopping’.
Fill in the Following details:
Full Name, Phone#, DOB, Email, Address, Country, Zip Postal Code, State/Province, City
And then press Confirm button to place your Order and get your Order#.
Once your order gets confirmed an email will be sent to your email ID.
After the confirmation, you will receive another email with a tracking number and your order details.
Upon receiving your order please check it and pay your due charges and keep your Invoice for at least 14 days (as per our return policy)
